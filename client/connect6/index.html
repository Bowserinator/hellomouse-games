Rules[1]

  1. Black plays first, putting one black stone on one intersection.
  2. White and Black take turns, placing two stones on two different unoccupied spaces each turn.
  3. Click "Submit Move" once you've placed your pieces.
  4. The player who first gets six or more stones in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.
